Birchermüesli

A perfect way to start the morning, this healthy breakfast cereal includes fresh fruit and yogurt!

1 serving

|

10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 Small Unpeeled Apples (such as Gala or Pink Lady)
  • 2 cups Rolled Oats
  • 2 cups Natural Yoghurt
  • 1 cup ml apple juice
  • 8 g Rolled Oats
  • Seasonal Berries or Fresh Fruit
  • Honey

Instructions

  1. Coarsely grate the apples (including the skin) into a large bowl.
  2. Add the rolled oats, yoghurt, and apple juice.
  3. Stir until all of the ingredients are combined.
  4. Wrap the bowl with some clingfilm.
  5. Place the bowl in the fridge overnight.
  6. To serve, the birchermüesli can be eaten as is, or with the addition with any fruits of your choice.
  7. Drizzle with some honey before serving.
