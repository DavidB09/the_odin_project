Birchermüesli

A perfect way to start the morning, this healthy breakfast cereal includes fresh fruit and yogurt!

1 servings

|

1 hour

Ingredients

  • 0.5 Banana
  • 1 Small Apple
  • 100 g Natural Yoghurt
  • 1-2 tbsp Milk
  • 8 g Rolled Oats
  • 8 g Ground Almonds
  • 1-2 tsp Raisins
  • 40 g Fresh or Frozen Raspberries

Instructions

  1. Peel 1/2 banana and mash with a fork. 1 Finely grate an apple with the skin on.
  2. Mix the banana and apple with 100 g plain yoghurt, 1-2 tablespoons of milk, 8 g each of oatmeal and ground almonds, 1-2 teaspoons of raisins and 40 g of fresh or frozen raspberries and leave to soak for at least 1 hour, but preferably overnight.
